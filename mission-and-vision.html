Mission & Vision Statement.

Mission Statement.

The mission of the Institute is to continue to operate as a centre of excellence through the provision of quality Technical, Vocational and Professional Education and Training thus contributing towards meeting the middle level human resource requirements of The Gambia.

We will continue our proactive response to the changing development needs, employment opportunities and realities of the nation and continue to aim at increasing our contribution towards national development by expanding the Institute’s range of training programmes to cater for all potential clients and the development of higher level programmes. In an attempt to address this challenge, GTTI will embark on working on both the Transformation and the Tertiary Integration processes to support the Institutional structures.

As part of the expansion process, GTTI will continue to cater for the expansion of skills training to the Rural Areas through its Rural Annex, the Rural Skills Training Centre (RSTC) in Mansakonko, Lower River Region in addition to the Banjul Skills Training Centre with a view to cater for the skills training needs of the Rural Community. The Centre has started since September 2008 operating and offering training programmes in Engineering and Construction.

Vision Statement.

The vision of the Institute is to efficiently and effectively contribute towards addressing the middle level human resource requirements of the Technical, Commercial and Industrial sectors of the country and the creation of opportunities for higher-level technical training through:

The provision of relevant and viable programmes that are sustainable and to engage in beneficial partnerships with local and international partners in order to ensure the Institute’s continuous survival.

The acquisition of well trained and highly qualified and motivated Gambian staffs that owns the process and can contribute effectively to the future development of the Institute.

The provision of access programmes for entry into higher-level Technical, Vocational and Professional courses offered by Higher Education Institutions and in particular the University of The Gambia.
