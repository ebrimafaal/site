STUDENTS' CLUB.

Enrichment.

We believe that school life is about developing a strong sense of character as well as academically. Therefore, in order to develop character learning we have a broad range of very popular extra curricular activities. Sport and physical activity is an important aspect to life at the school and our sports teams are recognised as some of the best in Gambia. We should try to have outstanding facilities including extensive fields, astro-turf, hard courts, sports hall, gymnasium, dance studio, swimming pool and fitness suite for students to enjoy. We have a very successful IT department and outstanding Ward programme, both departments offering a wide range of activity options. With high quality school drama productions that students can also get involved in. In addition to clubs and activities, many departments organise visits and residential trips which provide experiences that the students can draw on throughout their lives. In addition to exchange visits to senegal, we should also organize exchange visits to Mali, Ghana and Nageria, there are other residential trips to destinations such as Macharty island, fort bulen and James Island.

STUDENTS.

Exchanges & Trips

The Modern HND team has a uniquely successful exchange programme in that there are well subscribed exchanges with schools in MDI, UTG and Gambia Coluege.  Our partner schools are:-

  • University Sheikh Anta Jobe; Dakar.
  • University of the Gambia, Faraba.
  • Management Development Institute, Kanifing.

The exchange visits last for seven days and usually take place in February and March.  Other extra-curricular activities such as attending Language workshops and working with local primary schools are arranged as opportunities arise.
