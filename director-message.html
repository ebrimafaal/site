Director's Message.

The GTTI in Gambia started functionnin in 1983 with the offering of craft level programmes in a selection of technical and commercial disciplines and qualifications awarded buy the Royal society of arts (RSA), City and Guilds of London Institute (CGLI), & Pitman Training UK. Since 1983, the educational institute has broadened its curriculum from the craft level provision to full technological certificates; from Pitman and RSA programmes to AAT ABE and ACCA programmes in Business Studies; from manual short-hand to computerized stenography; from futher Educational teacher training to Higher training and during the last several years an HND in Building Construction Management taught in conjuction with the University of the Gambia and South Bank University in the UK.

Mrs Jahou Faal

The Board of Governors would like to extend its profound gratitude to the Government of the Republic of the Gambia through the Ministry of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology for the continuous support in the provision of the much needed subvention. However, the subvention has not been increasing over the years resulting in the institute’s limitations for expansion. As reported last year further growth will only be possible to be sustained if the subvention budget is increased to match the corresponding increase in staff and student size together with the institute’s expansion into the rural areas to minimize the rural urban drift.
